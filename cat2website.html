Welcome to Machakos Brew, our coffee paradise where every bean tells a story and every cup sparks joy.

We are a coffee shop located in Machakos, Kenya. We offer a variety of coffee blends and pastries to satisfy your cravings.

Our mission is to provide the best coffee experience to our customers.

We are open from 7 AM to 7 PM daily.
