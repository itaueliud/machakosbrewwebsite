About Machakos Grand

Hospitality shaped by Machakos warmth

Machakos Grand Hotel is a contemporary retreat rooted in Kenyan culture. Our story began with a desire to create a destination where business travelers and leisure guests find equal measure of calm and care.

From panoramic suites to chef-curated dining, every detail is designed to feel personal. Our team blends modern convenience with attentive service, ensuring each stay is effortless and memorable.

Explore the hills, enjoy rooftop evenings, and let us tailor the perfect itinerary for your time here.
